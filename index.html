About Me

I'm a content and social media marketer based in Bangalore with over 4 years of experience across marketing and client servicing. Most recently, I worked as Content and Growth Marketing Associate at WiseApp, a B2B SaaS platform for tutors and tutoring businesses, and previously as Marketing Analyst at Aikya Connect, India's investment network for single family offices.

I've had the opportunity to work across industries and am excited to bring that same energy to new spaces. What stays consistent is my approach: understanding what the brand stands for, who the audience is, and crafting content that actually resonates. I don't believe in one-size-fits-all content. Every brand has its own voice, and my job is to find it and build on it.

I specialise in content writing, content strategy, SEO content and optimisation, blog writing, content creation across LinkedIn and Instagram, and end-to-end campaign management.
